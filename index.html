Our Mission

Five Squared Interactive is a small company with big ambitions. We strive to provide the tools that will empower creatives and makers to build and share interactive experiences with the larger community.

The Web Metaverse is our present focus, and we're building a comprehensive suite of tools and platforms to make interactive 3D experiences accessible to everyone.
